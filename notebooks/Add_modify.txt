✅ New Columns Added (Phase 6 – Feature Engineering)

| New Column                   | Created From                             | Business Purpose                                           |
| ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| `age_group`                  | `age`                                    | Group employees by career stage (Young, Mid, Senior, etc.) |
| `salary_band`                | `monthly_income`                         | Classify employees into Low, Medium, and High income bands |
| `experience_level`           | `total_working_years`                    | Categorize employees by experience level                   |
| `promotion_due`              | `years_since_last_promotion`             | Identify employees who may be due for promotion            |
| `income_per_year_experience` | `monthly_income` + `total_working_years` | Measure salary relative to experience                      |
| `employee_tenure_category`   | `years_at_company`                       | Group employees by organizational tenure                   |
| `attrition_risk_score`       | Multiple HR factors                      | Estimate employee attrition risk using business rules      |
